• Exercising and feeling better

We’ve all experienced the sheer exhaustion that comes after strenuous activity – free weights, rowing machines, cardio machines, and such can really take it out of you.
But if you have done any hard exercise, you will also be familiar with the afterglow – that strange relaxed ‘feel good’ experience you get as your body starts to recover.
We now know that it’s due to chemicals being released in the body that help it to relax. It’s like your body’s reward system for helping it become healthier.

• The rise of the machines

A good fitness gym will be equipped with all the latest and modern gym health machines and ideally have someone specialising in biomedical science and the science of body building.
Examples of machines readily found in fitness centres include health machines like treadmills, cross trainers, upright bikes and rowers.
Health machines are not only modern and easy to use, but they also employ state of the art technology that reaps you immense health benefits.
